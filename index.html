Continuous monitoring powered by lightweight AI agents — spot anomalies and get explanations.

Live Dashboards

Customizable widgets, charts and logs — everything you need in one screen.

Automations

Run safe remediation scripts or send alerts to your team when thresholds are crossed.

About Chronix

Chronix started as a simple terminal utility and grew into a full cyber observability MVP. Built for fast diagnosis, clear triage, and easy automation — Chronix helps teams move from reactive to predictive.

Our mission

Make failure detection dependable and understandable using AI plus human readable reasons.

Roadmap

  • Better integrations (Slack, MS Teams, PagerDuty)
  • Custom automations and runbooks
  • Enterprise dashboards & role-based access
